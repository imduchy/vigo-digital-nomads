Our Story

We fell in love with Vigo and decided to stay

Hey there! We're a bunch of remote workers, freelancers, and digital nomads who've fallen in love with Vigo and decided to stick around — at least for a while.

This community is all about bringing together people from different corners of the world who work in or from Vigo and enjoy discovering new places. We started this in 2024 because Vigo needed a place where internationals could actually connect. Whether you're settling in for the long haul or just passing through, you'll find plenty of chances to meet new people and explore Galicia together.
