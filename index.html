About us

Welcome to the Vigo Digital Nomads and Expats Community! We are a group of remote workers, freelancers, and digital professionals who have chosen the beautiful city of Vigo, Spain as our base.

Our community brings together like-minded individuals from around the world who share a passion for location-independent work and exploring new cultures. Whether you're a long-term resident or just passing through, we provide opportunities to connect, collaborate, and make the most of your time in Galicia.

Our Community Values

Founded in 2024, the Vigo Digital Nomads and Expats Community was created to fill the gap for international professionals seeking connection in this vibrant coastal city. We embrace:

  • Inclusivity - welcoming digital professionals from all backgrounds, nationalities, and experience levels
  • Knowledge sharing - leveraging our collective expertise to help each other grow professionally
  • Cultural appreciation - embracing Galician culture while maintaining our global perspective
  • Meaningful engagement - fostering a respectful environment focused on collaboration and healthy activities rather than partying, excessive drinking, or flirting

Connect with us

We believe that meaningful connections enhance both personal and professional growth for locals, expats and digital nomadas alike. Our community offers multiple ways to engage:

  • Join our regular events through our Meetup.com group where we regularly organize events and casual gatherings
  • Connect instantly with our vibrant WhatsApp community group for real-time conversations, impromptu meetups, and local tips from established Vigo expats and locals
  • Participate in outdoor adventures including hiking the stunning coastal trails and beach days
